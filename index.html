About Me

Turning ideas into scalable software, intelligent automation and high-performance digital experiences.

Hello 👋

I'm Vishal Vishwakarma, a Software Engineer and Full Stack Developer focused on building scalable, high-performance digital products for growing businesses. Over the years I've worked across the stack - from crafting responsive interfaces in React and JavaScript, to architecting robust backend systems in PHP, to designing and integrating REST APIs that connect the tools businesses rely on every day.

My core specialisation is AI Automation - designing intelligent workflows that remove repetitive manual work, connect CRM and ERP systems, and let teams focus on higher-value work instead of admin. I've built enterprise applications, custom CRM platforms and internal dashboards that give operations teams real visibility into their business, and I bring the same rigor to public-facing products: SaaS platforms, WordPress enterprise solutions, and Shopify storefronts engineered for speed and conversion.

I care deeply about performance and user experience - a slow, confusing product costs businesses customers and money, so every system I build is optimised for clean architecture, fast load times and interfaces people actually enjoy using. I also spend a lot of time in the cloud, deploying, scaling and securing applications so they hold up under real traffic. Whether the challenge is automating a workflow with AI, building a CRM from scratch, modernising a legacy WordPress site, or architecting a new SaaS product end to end, my approach stays the same: understand the business problem first, then engineer a solution that's scalable, maintainable, and genuinely useful.
